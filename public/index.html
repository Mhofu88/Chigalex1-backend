FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Admin-added FAQs appear at the top tagged NEW. Core questions are below.

Is Pi Network free to join?▾
Yes — joining Pi Network is free. The Chigalex1 membership fee (1π) is an independent community app fee, not a charge by Pi Network or SocialChain Inc.
Do I need to keep the app open to mine Pi?▾
No. Pi mines in the background. You just need to tap the lightning button once every 24 hours to start a new session. No heavy battery or data usage.
My KYC document was rejected. What do I do?▾
Check the rejection reason shown in the app. Common issues: blurry photo, glare on the document, expired ID, or name mismatch with your Pi account. Fix the issue and resubmit with good lighting on a flat surface.
Can a business do KYB without the owner completing personal KYC?▾
No. The business owner or authorized representative must first complete personal KYC before submitting a KYB application. Both are required for full business verification.
When can I spend or withdraw my Pi?▾
Pi can be spent with KYB-verified businesses that accept Pi. The Open Mainnet (for external trading) is progressively rolling out — KYC-verified pioneers access it first.
What is Map of Pi and why should my business list there?▾
mapofpi.com is the official Pi business directory on a live map. 50+ million pioneers use it to find places to spend Pi. Listing is free after KYB approval and gives your business global and local visibility.
Is Pi Network available in my SADC country?▾
Yes — Pi Network is available in all 16 SADC member states: Angola, Botswana, Comoros, DRC, Eswatini, Lesotho, Madagascar, Malawi, Mauritius, Mozambique, Namibia, Seychelles, South Africa, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.
How do I report a Pi Network scam?▾
Report via the Pi app's support section, email support@minepi.com, or contact @PiCoreTeam on Twitter/X. Never send Pi to anyone claiming to multiply it or pretending to be Pi staff.
